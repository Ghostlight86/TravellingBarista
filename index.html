About Us

Founded with a passion for exceptional coffee and unparalleled service, Travelling Barista is your mobile coffee solution for any occasion. Our skilled baristas are trained in the art of coffee preparation, ensuring every cup is a masterpiece.

We bring our fully-equipped mobile coffee station to your location, serving a variety of specialty drinks made with ethically sourced beans and premium ingredients.

Whether it's a corporate event, wedding, private party, or office gathering, we elevate your experience with the perfect cup of coffee.
