Our Story

Founded in the heart of Bloemfontein, Travelling Barista brings the warmth and aroma of specialty coffee directly to your events. Our passionate baristas are dedicated to crafting the perfect cup of coffee, tailored to your preferences.

Whether it's a corporate meeting, wedding celebration, or private party, we transform any venue into an inviting coffee haven with our rustic mobile coffee station and expert service.

We source our beans from ethical suppliers and roast them to perfection, ensuring every sip delivers the rich, complex flavors that coffee lovers crave.
